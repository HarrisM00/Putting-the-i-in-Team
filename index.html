Justice League

Batman

A member of the Justice League and and iconic hero in DC Comics. His real identity is Bruce Wayne, a wealthy businessman. He dedicated his life to fighting crime after the murder of his parents.

Superman

Also known as Man of Steel and Clark Kent. Born on another planet called Krypton, he was sent to Earth as a baby. He has superpowers such as flight, super strength, and laser vision.

Wonder Woman

Also known as Diana Prince. She is from the island of Themyscira. She has superpowers such as superhuman strength, agility, and the ability to fly. She left her homeland to fight for justice.

The Flash

Also known as Barry Allen. His main superpower is super speed. He can travel through time.

Cyborg

Cyborg is also known as Victor Stone. He possess superhuman strength and has built in weapons and gadgets. His father who is a scientist replaced his damaged body with cybernetic technology to save his life.

Aquaman

He is also known as Arthur Curry. He is the king of the underwater kingdom of Atlantis. He has superpowers such as breathing underwater and talking with marine animals. He has superhuman strength and can swim fast. He tries to protect the ocean world and the surface.
